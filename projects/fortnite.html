Fortnite × Times Square Digital Experience

A large-scale interactive Fortnite experience tied to the Times Square New Year’s Eve celebration, designed to drive engagement and online reach.

Overview

This project involved contributing to a Fortnite-based digital experience that connected online players with a real-world global event. The experience gained significant traction across social platforms and reached millions of viewers.

My Role

  • Contributed to the planning and execution of an interactive Fortnite experience
  • Worked with a broader team to align the experience with event timing and audience engagement
  • Helped support the rollout and visibility of the experience across digital channels

Tools & Platforms

Fortnite Creative Digital Event Marketing Cross-platform Engagement Team Collaboration

Outcome

The project achieved over 2 million views on social platforms and demonstrated how digital interactive spaces can amplify real-world events. It strengthened my interest in experiential digital products and large-scale audience engagement.
