Creators of Spider-Man

From Stan Lee and Steve Ditko in the 1960s to modern writers and artists, these are the voices and visions that built Spider-Man’s world.

Essential Spider-Man Creators

Stan Lee

Co-Creator · Writer · Editor

Era: 1960s–1970s (Marvel’s Silver Age)

Co-created Spider-Man in Amazing Fantasy #15 (1962). Gave Peter Parker his voice: quippy, anxious, and deeply human. Lee’s run introduced Aunt May, J. Jonah Jameson, the Green Goblin, Doctor Octopus, and the idea that “with great power comes great responsibility.”

  • Key issues: AF #15 · ASM #1–100

Steve Ditko

Co-Creator · Penciler

Era: 1960s

Designed Spider-Man’s costume, web-swinging style, and many of his most iconic villains. Ditko’s moody cityscapes and awkward teen body language sold the idea of a kid superhero juggling homework and heroism.

  • Key issues: AF #15 · ASM #1–38

John Romita Sr.

Artist · Co-Plotter

Era: Late 1960s–1970s

Took over after Ditko and gave Spider-Man a more polished, romantic look. Helped define Peter’s supporting cast, including Gwen Stacy and Mary Jane Watson, and drew many of the series’ most famous covers.

  • Key issues: ASM #39–100

Todd McFarlane

Artist · Writer

Era: Late 1980s–Early 1990s

Brought a hyper-dynamic, stylized Spider-Man with huge eyes and “spaghetti webbing.” His art helped redefine the character for a new generation and boosted Spider-Man to massive sales.

  • Key issues: ASM #298–325 · Spider-Man (1990) #1–14

Brian Michael Bendis

Writer · Co-Creator of Miles Morales

Era: 2000s–2010s (Ultimate Universe)

Relaunched Peter Parker for modern readers in Ultimate Spider-Man, then co-created Miles Morales. Bendis focused on character-driven stories, teen dialogue, and long-form arcs that inspired later films and games.

  • Key runs: Ultimate Spider-Man · Ultimate Comics: Spider-Man

Dan Slott

Writer

Era: 2000s–2010s (Longest-Running Spidey Writer)

Wrote Spider-Man for over a decade, steering huge status-quo changes: Big Time, Spider-Island, Superior Spider-Man, and the return of the classic Peter/MJ dynamic.

  • Key runs: ASM (vol. 2 & 3) · Superior Spider-Man
