Sources & References

This project is a fan-made overview of Spider-Man’s history across comics, films, animation, and games. The pages draw on official Marvel material, fan-maintained databases, and entertainment references for images, dates, and story summaries.

Below is a selection of the main sources used. All characters, logos, and storylines are trademarks of Marvel and their respective rights holders.
