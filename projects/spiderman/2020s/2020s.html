2020s – Multiverse, Reboots, & Massive Change

The 2020s redefine Spider-Man across comics, films, animation, and video games. From No Way Home breaking reality open, to Ben Reilly’s return, to modern events like Dark Web and Gang War, this decade pushes Spider-Man’s world to its biggest scale ever.

Milestones

The defining Spider-Man stories of the 2020s — reality breaks, massive crossovers, legacy heroes rising, and multiverse-shifting hits.

Years 2020–2029
Key creators Nick Spencer, Zeb Wells, Patrick Gleason, Cody Ziglar
Tone Multiverse, legacy, mystery arcs, high-stakes reinventions

Film · Multiverse

Spider-Man: No Way Home (2021)

The multiverse cracks open, uniting Tom Holland, Andrew Garfield, and Tobey Maguire in a crossover that becomes a global phenomenon.

Film · MCU

Spider-Man: Brand New Day (2026)

Release date: July 31, 2026 · Starring: Tom Holland, Zendaya

The start of a new MCU trilogy. After the world forgets Peter Parker’s identity, Peter leans fully into being Spider-Man with no civilian safety net, exploring what it means to be a hero when you’ve truly lost everything else.

TV · Animation

Your Friendly Neighborhood Spider-Man (2025– )

Disney+ animated series · Showrunner: Jeff Trammell

A cel-shaded origin-era series set in an alternate MCU timeline where Norman Osborn mentors Peter instead of Tony Stark. The show leans into early Ditko/Romita vibes and has already been renewed for multiple seasons with more Spider-variants on the way.

Comics · Legacy

Amazing Spider-Man: Beyond (2021–2022)

Ben Reilly steps back into the role of Spider-Man, backed by the shady Beyond Corporation. His new identity crisis sets up major consequences for Peter and Ben.

Event

Dark Web (2022–2023)

Ben Reilly becomes Chasm and teams with Madelyne Pryor, launching a surreal demonic invasion of New York.

Film · Animation

Across the Spider-Verse (2023)

An artistic, emotional expansion of the Spider-Verse. Introduces Spider-Man 2099, Spider-Punk, and hundreds of variants.

Video Game

Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 (2023)

Peter and Miles face Kraven and Venom in one of the most cinematic superhero games ever created.

Event

Gang War (2024)

A street-level war erupts between NYC’s crime families, forcing Peter, Miles, Spider-Woman, and others to form new alliances to save the city.

Comics

Amazing Spider-Man (2022–present) – Zeb Wells

A controversial run exploring mystery arcs, broken relationships, and redefining Peter’s personal life and hero role.

Film · Upcoming

Beyond the Spider-Verse (TBA)

The conclusion to Miles Morales’ animated trilogy, set to resolve cliffhangers and expand the multiverse further.

2020s Quick Notes

  • Multiverse dominance: Movies, animation, and even comics fully embrace alternate Spider-heroes and universe-breaking plots.
  • Ben Reilly’s reinvention: From Spider-Man to corporate hero to Chasm, his arc becomes central to early-2020s storytelling.
  • Zeb Wells era: A divisive but bold take focusing on mystery, tension, and Peter’s complicated personal life.
  • Miles Morales rises: Miles becomes one of Marvel’s most important characters thanks to film, games, and comics.
  • Spider-Man 2 (PS5): Sets a new standard for superhero storytelling in games with emotional stakes and cinematic combat.
  • Gang War era: A return to street-level storytelling with modern crime factions battling for NYC control.
  • Cross-media domination: Comics, movies, TV, animation, and gaming all push Spider-Man to new cultural highs.
